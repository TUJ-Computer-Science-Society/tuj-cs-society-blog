# Welcome!

Welcome to Temple University Japan Computer Science Society's very own Blog! 
On this blog, we'll write about events, networking opportunities, career opportunities, personal event experiences, personal excerpts from CS Society members, and lots more!

# Community and Social Media

TUJ CS Society has a lot of ways you can communicate and contribute to the society and community at TUJ. Here are a few of them:

[![Join our Community Discord Server](https://gist.github.com/cxmeel/0dbc95191f239b631c3874f4ccf114e2/raw/discord.svg)](https://discord.gg/aP4KbbazJu) [![Follow our Instagram](https://gist.github.com/cxmeel/0dbc95191f239b631c3874f4ccf114e2/raw/instagram.svg)](https://www.instagram.com/tuj_cs_society/)

You can also follow us on LinkedIn for professional updates, job applications, and more: [TUJ CS Society LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/company/computer-science-academic-society-tuj/)

Want to stay upto date with society projects and this Blog's Repository? Follow our GitHub account.

[![Follow our GitHub](https://gist.github.com/cxmeel/0dbc95191f239b631c3874f4ccf114e2/raw/github_source.svg)](https://github.com/TUJ-Computer-Science-Society)

Do you have any questions or concerns? 

Email us at [TUJ CS Society's Official Mail](mailto:tujcsacademicsociety@gmail.com)
